About me

Hello! My name is Emily Simons and I'm a Front-End Developer student based in The Netherlands.

I'm a big fan of working and being creative, when it comes to programming I like keeping it minimalistic and simple. There's just something about taking a canvas and turning it into something. It's like being an artist with a keyboard and screen.

What really gets my creative flowing are games, movies, and anime. I find inspiration in the immersive worlds, intricate storylines, and stunning visuals they offer. Whether it's the captivating narratives of movies or the fantastical realms of anime.

That's why I'm particularly drawn to the front-end side of programming. It's where I get to blend my love for aesthetics and user experience. Crafting responsive websites and interactive interfaces feels like sculpting a unique experience for every visitor.
